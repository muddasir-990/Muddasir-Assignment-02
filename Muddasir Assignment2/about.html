  5. Health Benefits Encourages physical activity, even if it's lighter than a normal bike. Riding outdoors helps reduce stress and improve mental well-being.
  6. Time-Saving Faster than regular bicycles with less fatigue. Allows longer rides without getting tired.
  7. Energy Independence Reduces dependence on imported fuels. Encourages the use of local energy sources, especially if renewable.
  8. Better for Roads and Traffic Lighter weight means less damage to roads. Helps reduce overall traffic congestion if more people use bikes instead of cars.
